Privacy Policy

TADucation is committed to protecting your privacy under the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) of the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth). This policy explains how we handle your personal information.

1. What is Personal Information?

Personal information identifies an individual, whether true or not, and regardless of format.

2. Collection of Personal Information

We collect personal information directly from you when you use our services, fill out forms, or communicate with us. This includes your name, email, mobile number, academic history, and career goals.

We may also collect non-personal data like browser type and IP address for analytics.

3. Purpose of Collection

We collect your information to:

  • Provide educational consultancy and mentoring.
  • Respond to your inquiries.
  • Communicate service updates and opportunities.
  • Improve our website and services.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. Use and Disclosure of Personal Information

We use and disclose your information only for collected purposes, related secondary purposes, or as required by law.

We may share your information with:

  • Third-party service providers (e.g., IT, payment processors).
  • Universities/institutions (with your consent).
  • Government bodies (if legally required).

Overseas Disclosure

We may disclose information to overseas recipients (e.g., educational institutions) relevant to your study aspirations. We take reasonable steps to ensure they comply with APPs.

5. Storage and Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from misuse, loss, and unauthorized access, using secure servers and access controls.

6. Access and Correction

You can request access to or correction of your personal information. Contact us to do so.

7. Marketing Communications

You may receive marketing communications from us. You can opt-out anytime via the unsubscribe link or by contacting us.

8. Complaints

For privacy complaints, contact us in writing. We will investigate and respond. If unsatisfied, you may contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC).

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. The latest version will be posted here.

10. Contact Us

For privacy questions, contact us at: info@taducation.com.
